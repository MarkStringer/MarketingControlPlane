I'm doing two (at least) work in progress shows in London before I go up to perform at the Fringe. This show isn't about me. It's about you. It's about how you can write a book. They say everyone has got a book in them. They don't say it won't be painful to get it out! This show starts you on that journey as painlessly as possible.

On the way, I tell you a bit about my own journey and throw in hints, tips and advice gathered from other writers. I also talk about the things nobody told me, the things I had to find out for myself.

WIP shows are FREE - 50 minutes.
The Cavendish Arms, Stockwell, London.
Tuesday June 16 and Tuesday June 30, 9pm.
