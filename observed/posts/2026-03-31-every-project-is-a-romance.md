# Post text

David Foster Wallace said every love story is a ghost story. I think every project is a romance.
The classic (supposed) reasons for getting married — money, love, revenge — are pretty good reasons for a project to happen too.

And then there are the disaster movie drama reasons.

Burning platforms. Sinking ships.

Fear that if this doesn't happen, something worse will.

There's almost always some drama behind why a project exists. It's a good idea to find out what it is.
