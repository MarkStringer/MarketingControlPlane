Ahmed - good list! But the skill I'd add is the willingness to say what's actually happening. Enthusiasm without honesty just delays the bad news. And how you handle bad news is what separates a project that survives from one that doesn't.

Oh, and advocate for the PROJECT. The team? Yes, you protect them. You go to bat for them. But you look after the team, because that looks after the project, and of course, in the end that's the best thing for the customer too.
