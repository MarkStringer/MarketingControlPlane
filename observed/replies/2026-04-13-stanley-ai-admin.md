I think AI will just allow you to back up with data the intuitions the "old 80%" barely touched. The interesting question is whether that's easier or harder. Will it make things more or less pleasant. I'm starting to think that the real kernel of project management is having a particular attitude to bad news - treating it as data - and working with clients to turn that data into information, knowledge and eventually action. AI will give us a ton more data - which means it will also give us a lot more bad news, much faster - and with receipts.
