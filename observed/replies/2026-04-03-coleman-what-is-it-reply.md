# Reply text

Thanks Joanna. I completely agree with this idea of varying between "structure" and "flexibility."

There's this thing from Taiichi Ohno in his book "The Toyota production system" which always haunts me. He says you should always be asking "What is it?"

And I worry that it's the project manager's job to know and communicate this! What ARE we doing now? Is that what we SHOULD be doing?

Did the game just change? Is it about something else now?

Obviously this is a really difficult question that we will often struggle to answer correctly. But I think it's probably the thing we should always be doing - answering the question "What is it?" OK, we departed from our stated process. Now what?
