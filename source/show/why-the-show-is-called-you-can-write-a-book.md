# Why the Show is Called "You Can Write a Book"

*Video transcript, 2026-03-27*

Okay, so here's the basic situation. I have a book coming out. I thought it'd come out, but now it hasn't. That's not a basic situation. It's a complicated situation. But anyway, I don't know how to market it, and I'm going to do a show at the Edinburgh Festival, and my first idea for the show was to call it, please buy my book, because I don't know how to market my book. But then I thought, well, that's a bit — it's a bit on the nose, so to speak. So maybe there's a better way of kind of talking to people about my book, but without directly forcing them to buy it. And so the show is called "You Can Write a Book." And the idea is that within the 45 minutes allotted to a fringe show, you can start to get some ideas about how to write a book.
