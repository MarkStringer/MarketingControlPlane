I'm doing two (at least) work in progress shows in London before I go up to perform at the Fringe.

This show isn't about me. It's about you. It's about how you can write a book.

They say everyone has got a book in them. They don't say it won't be painful to get it out.

This show starts you on that journey. On the way, I tell you a bit about my own journey and throw in hints, tips and advice gathered from other writers. I also talk about the things nobody told me, the things I had to find out for myself.

Free. 50 minutes. The Cavendish Arms, Stockwell, London. June 16 and June 30, 9pm.
