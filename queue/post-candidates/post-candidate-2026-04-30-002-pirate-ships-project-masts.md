# Pirate Ships & Project Masts

## Hook

Raise your mast and sail through project uncertainty like a pirate!

## Rationale

## Draft

Imagine your software project as a pirate ship, braving unpredictable seas. The mast? It’s your project plan—vital, but not absolute. Pirate ships thrive by adapting to the wind and listening for hidden dangers. Similarly, the real treasure in project management lies in surfacing risks early and steering through them, rather than clinging blindly to the original course. *Delivering the Impossible* uses this analogy to prepare you for the unpredictable journey of software delivery. Change course if needed and hunt down those hidden opportunities.

## Why now
