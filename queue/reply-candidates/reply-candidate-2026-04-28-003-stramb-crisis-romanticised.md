I'd push back slightly.

The Friday 5PM crisis is usually visible on Thursday morning. Sometimes Tuesday. It comes from bad news that nobody wanted to hear earlier in the week, so it stayed bad news until it became a crisis.

The goal isn't to be good at handling the Friday 5PM crisis. The goal is to have fewer of them.

People will remember how you handled the crisis. But they'll also remember that there was one. Delivery that doesn't generate crises is quiet and boring and much easier to forget. It's also, all things considered, better.

Bad news is data. By the time it's a Friday 5PM crisis, the data was there all along — it just wasn't being treated as data.
