# Draft reply

"Soft skills" is a strange name for what you're actually describing. Communication and decision-making under pressure are only soft until the moment you have to walk into a room and tell a senior stakeholder that the project is behind, over budget, and the scenario they've been briefed on isn't the likely one. At that point it stops feeling soft.

The real differentiator is more specific: the willingness to hold an accurate picture of reality when everyone around you wants a comfortable fiction. AI will give us better data, faster — which means it will also generate bad news at speed, with receipts. The PM who can absorb that, synthesize it honestly, and walk into the room anyway — that's the differentiator. Certifications don't test for it. Neither does a course on "influence."
