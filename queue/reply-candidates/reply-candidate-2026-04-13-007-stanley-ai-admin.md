The 80/20 split is interesting but worth pushing on what "high-value" actually means. Client conversations and prioritisation decisions aren't valuable because they're strategic — they're valuable because that's where the uncertainty lives. You can't automate uncertainty away. The 80% you want to eliminate is mostly the paperwork generated by not having resolved the hard conversations in the first place.
