"Robust complexity is not invented; it is grown." That is a good description of how complex systems actually work.

The problem is that sponsors do not run on Gall's Law. They run on quarterly cycles and launch dates already in press releases.

Someone has to stand in front of the people holding the budget and explain why the validated-fragment approach will take longer to show results than they want. That conversation is the real constraint on any iterative method. The methodology does not negotiate with the board.
